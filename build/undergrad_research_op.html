ReRout Lab: Undergrad Research Opportunity

ReRout Lab, the research group led by Dr. Christopher Stewart, is looking to increase undergraduate involvement in research. Are you interested in drones, cloud computing, data analytics, deep learning or STEM education? If yes, consider attending our group meetings and doing a report on a published paper in your area of interest. You will get paid for attending meetings, studying published work and learning about your passion.

You will need to attend our group meetings and select a paper to present (examples are listed below). You will work with Dr. Stewart and a PhD student to prepare a presentation. Our meetings are Wednesdays from 2:00 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. in DL 698. It is mandatory that you attend group meetings though arriving late or leaving earlier can be allowed. You will get paid for 15 hours of work across semester (attending at least 5 meetings, preparing a presentation and presenting at another meeting). You will be paid at the rate of $10/hr. We have some flexibility to increase pay if students select challenging papers for presentation or achieve stellar attendance and participation.

How to apply

  1. First, fill the departamental undergraduate research application (available here)
  2. Then, fill out this google form, indicating which of the following papers you would like to present.

Paper:

  • Accelerating Large Scale Deep Learning Inference through DeepCPU at Microsoft
  • A Comparative Measurement Study of Deep Learning as a Service Framework
  • The Queuing-First Approach for Tail Management of Interactive Services
  • Invariant time-series factorization
  • A Complexity-Invariant Distance Measure for Time Series
  • In-situ AI:Towards Autonomous and Incremental Deep Learning for IoT Systems
  • On-demand Deep Model Compression for Mobile Devices:A Usage-Driven Model Selection Framework
  • Evaluating Multispectral Images and Vegetation Indices for Precision Farming Applications from UAV Images
  • Estimating Biomass of Barley Using Crop Surface Models (CSMs) Derived from UAV-Based RGB Imaging
  • FarmBeats:An IoT Platform for Data-Driven Agriculture
  • MAVBench:Micro Aerial Vehicle Benchmarking
